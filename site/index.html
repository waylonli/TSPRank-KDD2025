TSPRank

Bridging pairwise and listwise ranking with a bilinear Travelling Salesman model.

TSPRank learns pairwise transition scores between ranking entities, converts them into a directed graph, and uses TSP optimization to recover a globally coherent listwise ranking.

Weixian Waylon Li · Yftah Ziser · Yifei Xie · Shay B. Cohen · Tiejun Ma

Method

Ranking as graph optimization

The model takes embeddings from a Transformer encoder or another backbone model, scores pairwise directed edges with a trainable bilinear form, and ranks entities by solving for the best route through the graph.

01

Pairwise modeling

Scores directed entity pairs, preserving inter-entity relationships that pointwise methods cannot express.

02

Listwise decoding

Uses TSP inference to choose one coherent ordering rather than independent pairwise wins.

03

Backbone compatible

Works as an additional ranking component on top of domain-specific embeddings.
